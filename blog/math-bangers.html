Recent work in neuroscience has gotten me excited about pure math again, so I decided to relive some good times and catalogue some of the coolest things I've encountered in math. Hopefully this will also help me out whenever I have to explain to friends, family, or strangers why I think math is so beautiful, or when I have an opportunity to work with and try to inspire kids - I don't know why, but I've always drawn a blank in these situations!
